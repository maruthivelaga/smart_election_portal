Candidate Manifesto

Sarah Johnson

Candidate for SAC President

My Vision for the Student Body

As your Student Activity Council President, I am committed to creating a more inclusive, transparent, and active campus community. My vision centers around three key pillars:

1. Transparent Governance

I will implement monthly open forums where all students can directly voice concerns to SAC representatives. Additionally, I'll create a public budget dashboard showing how student funds are allocated and spent.

2. Inclusive Campus Culture

I plan to establish a Diversity and Inclusion Committee within SAC to ensure all campus events and initiatives represent our diverse student population. We'll increase funding for cultural celebrations and create spaces for underrepresented groups.

3. Mental Health Advocacy

I will work with the university administration to expand mental health resources, including peer support programs and stress-relief events during exam periods. Every student deserves access to the support they need to thrive academically and personally.

Qualifications

  • Vice President of Computer Science Society (2022-2023)
  • Student Representative on University Welfare Committee
  • Organized Campus Tech Week with 500+ attendees
  • Peer Mentor for first-year students
