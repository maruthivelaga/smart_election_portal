Voter Guidelines

Who is eligible to vote in campus elections?

All currently enrolled students with a valid student ID are eligible to vote in campus elections. You must be registered for classes in the current semester and have no outstanding disciplinary actions against you.

How do I verify my voter eligibility?

You can verify your eligibility by logging into the campus election portal with your student credentials. The system will automatically check your enrollment status and display your voting eligibility.

What is the voting period?

The voting period typically lasts for 48 hours. Exact dates and times are announced at least two weeks in advance through official campus channels. Once the voting period ends, no further votes can be cast.

Can I change my vote after submitting?

No, votes are final once submitted. The system is designed to prevent any changes after submission to maintain election integrity. Please review your selections carefully before finalizing your vote.

Candidate Information

How do I submit my nomination?

Nominations must be submitted through the official campus election portal during the designated nomination period. You'll need to complete the nomination form, upload a profile photo, and provide a brief manifesto.

What are the campaign rules?

Campaigning must adhere to campus policies. Key rules include: no defamation of opponents, no campaigning within 50 feet of polling stations, all campaign materials must be approved, and digital campaigning must follow social media guidelines.

When will election results be announced?

Results are typically announced within 24 hours after voting closes. All candidates will receive notification before the public announcement. Official results are posted on the campus election website.

Troubleshooting

I can't log in to vote. What should I do?

First, ensure you're using your correct campus credentials. If you've forgotten your password, use the password reset tool. If issues persist, contact the IT help desk with your student ID and a description of the error message you're receiving.

The voting page isn't loading properly. How can I fix this?

Try these steps: 1) Refresh the page, 2) Clear your browser cache, 3) Try a different browser, 4) Check your internet connection. If the problem continues, try accessing the voting portal from a different device or campus computer.

I received an error message after submitting my vote. Did my vote count?

The system provides a confirmation message when your vote is successfully recorded. If you received an error, your vote may not have been processed. Try voting again. The system prevents duplicate voting, so your original vote will still count if it was received.

How do I report election irregularities?

Any concerns about election integrity should be reported immediately to the Election Committee via email at elections@campus.edu or in person at the Student Affairs office. Please provide as much detail as possible about the incident.
